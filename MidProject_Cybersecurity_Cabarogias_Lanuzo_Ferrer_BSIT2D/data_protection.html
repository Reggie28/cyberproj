Data Protection

Data protection is crucial to safeguarding personal and business information from breaches, theft, and loss.

Best Practices for Data Protection:

  • Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
  • Encrypt sensitive data before storing or sharing.
  • Regularly update software to patch vulnerabilities.
  • Be cautious with public Wi-Fi networks.
  • Back up important files frequently.

Common Data Protection Threats & Solutions

Threat Solution
Phishing Attacks Verify links before clicking & use email filters.
Malware & Ransomware Use antivirus software & avoid suspicious downloads.
Data Breaches Use strong passwords & encrypt sensitive data.
Unsecured Wi-Fi Use VPNs for secure internet connections.
