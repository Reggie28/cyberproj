Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from cyber threats, unauthorized access, and cyber-attacks. It plays a crucial role in ensuring the safety of personal, financial, and confidential information.

üîë Key Cybersecurity Practices

  • Use Strong Passwords: Create complex passwords with a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters.
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication: Adds an extra security layer to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Update Software Regularly: Keep your software and operating system up to date to protect against vulnerabilities.
  • Beware of Phishing Scams: Do not click on suspicious links or share personal information online.
  • Use Antivirus Software: Install trusted security software to detect and remove potential threats.
  • Secure Your Wi-Fi: Use a strong password and enable WPA3 encryption to protect your network.
  • Limit Personal Information Sharing: Be mindful of sharing personal details on social media to prevent identity theft.
